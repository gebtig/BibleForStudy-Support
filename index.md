## About BibleForStudy

BibleForStudy is a verse-by-verse Bible study app designed especially for iPad users who want to write, reflect, and study Scripture deeply.

Included translations:
- King James Version (Public Domain)
- American Standard Version (Public Domain)
- World English Bible (Public Domain)

---

## Privacy

BibleForStudy does not collect, store, or share personal data.

All notes and highlights are stored locally on your device.
